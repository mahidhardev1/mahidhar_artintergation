Bharatanatyam

About the Dance

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dances from Tamil Nadu. It is known for expressive gestures, rhythmic footwork, and storytelling.

Origin

It originated in ancient temples and was performed by Devadasis as a form of spiritual expression.

Best Known Dancer

Rukmini Devi Arundale is widely regarded as one of the greatest Bharatanatyam dancers in history.

Features

  • Sharp and graceful movements
  • Strong focus on facial expressions
  • Detailed hand gestures (mudras)
  • Traditional Carnatic music
